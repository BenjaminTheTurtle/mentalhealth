    Some of you might be wondering the purpose of this website, so we will be answering it.

    Well, we have been noticing that more and more people, youths in general are facing alot of issues regarding their psychological status yet this problem doesn't get enough importance making it get underestimated. So, by creating this website, more and more users should be aware of how our mental health is affected in everyday life and also about the importance of good mental health. Additionally, we too would like more people to know about how we can help people suffering from mental issues. At first glance, most people might have bad impressions on someone that is acting involunterily because of their mental health but we should always try to think in a better perspective and see if they are okay or not by comforting them.

    We are all glad that his website and this project turned out succesful. Hopefully, more people will know about this global issue that is mental health not just in our local commmunity but to extend it around the world.
